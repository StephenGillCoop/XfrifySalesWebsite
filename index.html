About us

VME Retail Systems Ltd have been trading over 25 years, providing our own high quality epos system (IntelliStore) to convenience stores and supermarkets throughout the UK. We focus on grocery epos only (including forecourts), which makes us one of the strongest suppliers in our niche market.

Our software is written and maintained by ourselves, using the latest Microsoft .Net technology. We have a strong development team producing software enhancements every day, and a large support team to assist our customers using our system to run their businesses.
